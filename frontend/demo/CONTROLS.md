# Civicverse Demo - Player Controls

## Movement Controls

### Primary Controls
- **W** - Move forward
- **A** - Strafe left
- **S** - Move backward
- **D** - Strafe right

### Alternative Controls
- **Arrow Up** - Move forward
- **Arrow Left** - Strafe left
- **Arrow Down** - Move backward
- **Arrow Right** - Strafe right

## Camera
- **Mouse Drag** - Rotate camera around player (OrbitControls)
- **Scroll** - Camera follows player automatically

## Character
- **Character Model**: Articulated player avatar with torso, head, 2 arms, and 2 legs
- **Animation**: Limbs animate smoothly when moving
- **Facing**: Character faces the direction of movement

## UI
- **Left Sidebar**: Module selection buttons (Dashboard, Voting, DEX, etc.)
- **3D City**: Main view with real-time rendered environment
- **HUD Overlay**: Player stats and game information

## Environment
- **7 Detailed Buildings**: High-quality procedural buildings with window grids
- **Street Lights**: 12 ambient street lamps with warm glow
- **Plaza Fountain**: Animated water feature at city center
- **Billboard**: Rotating screen showing crypto prices (KAS/XMR)
- **Ground Grid**: Street layout with 20-unit spacing

## Tips
- Press **W** to start moving and watch your character animate
- Use **A/D** to strafe while moving forward for fluid navigation
- Drag with mouse to rotate camera and see the city from different angles
- Use module buttons to interact with Civicverse features while viewing the 3D world
